About

I'm a systems-oriented product designer who thrives in complex, ambiguous environments. I'm drawn to the edges of definition — the places where people don't yet have clarity.

My work sits at the intersection of systems and humanity. I build platforms and internal tools that scale, but I'm equally motivated by the environments people work and learn inside. I've seen how poorly designed systems can make people feel alone in uncertainty — and I design to counter that.

I actively use AI as a thinking partner, exploring human-in-the-loop systems that support judgment rather than override it. I'm particularly interested in how technology can reduce knowledge friction while preserving transparency, trust, and human agency.

I care deeply about the question underneath it all: how can we build technology that brings people together instead of quietly pulling them apart?
